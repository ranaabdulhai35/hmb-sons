ZTBL Tractor Financing

As an authorized vendor, we supply tractors and machinery against ZTBL's tractor loan scheme

📋

What is financed

Purchase of new tractors and vehicles for transporting agricultural produce from farm to market, under the Zarai Taraqiati Bank Limited (ZTBL) scheme.

💰

Loan terms

  • Up to Rs. 2.5 million per borrower
  • Repayment over 8 years (half-yearly installments)
  • 10% down payment by the borrower
  • Comprehensive insurance included
🤝

How we help

Our team assists with documentation, vendor invoicing and coordination with ZTBL branches — so you get your machinery with minimum hassle.
