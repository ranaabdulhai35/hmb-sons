ZTBL Enlistment & Financing

As an authorized vendor, we supply tractors and machinery against ZTBL's tractor loan scheme

📜

Officially Enlisted with ZTBL

M/s HMB & Sons is enlisted and approved by Zarai Taraqiati Bank Limited's General Services Department for the provision and installation of farm and agricultural machinery, agri land development through alternate and renewable energy, and green banking products to ZTBL field offices across Pakistan.

Enlistment Letter No. GSD/Enlistment/Machinery/2024/64, dated 29 January 2024. Instructions issued to all ZTBL field offices vide Circular No. SD/03/2023/03, with demand drafts issued directly to enlisted firms.

📋

What is financed

Purchase of new tractors and vehicles for transporting agricultural produce from farm to market, under the Zarai Taraqiati Bank Limited (ZTBL) scheme.

💰

Loan terms

  • Up to Rs. 2.5 million per borrower
  • Repayment over 8 years (half-yearly installments)
  • 10% down payment by the borrower
  • Comprehensive insurance included
🤝

How we help

Our team assists with documentation, vendor invoicing and coordination with ZTBL branches, so you get your machinery with minimum hassle.

Source: ztbl.com.pk (Tractor Loan)
