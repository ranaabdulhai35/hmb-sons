Seeds

Certified seed from Punjab Seed Corporation, the foundation of a healthy, high-yield crop

Why certified seed?

All seed we supply from Punjab Seed Corporation (PSC) is duly certified by the Federal Seed Certification & Registration Department, guaranteeing purity, germination and varietal identity under PSC's trusted Shahzore brand.

  • Higher and more uniform germination
  • True-to-type varieties with disease screening
  • Better response to fertilizer and water
  • Backed by PSC quality testing laboratories
