  • ☀️ Solar system financing
  • ⚡ Fast processing & approvals

Zarai Taraqiati Bank Limited (ZTBL)

Tractor & farm development loans

Pakistan's premier agricultural development bank. We supply tractors and machinery under ZTBL's tractor loan scheme — up to Rs. 2.5 million per borrower, repayable over 8 years with a 10% down payment and comprehensive insurance.

  • 🚜 Tractor loans (up to Rs. 2.5M)
  • 🌱 Kissan Khushhal production loans
  • 🐄 Dairy, poultry & farm mechanisation schemes

Loan approval rests with the respective bank. Our team assists with documentation, vendor invoicing and coordination — contact us to get started.
